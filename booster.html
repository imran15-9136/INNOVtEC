Restaurant Website

A web base application.

This application you can use as your restaurants website. User can register your website & also can order food. Customer have to add to cart form menu list & then can make payment to stripe online payment gateway. Customer also can booking table for special purpose. Restaurant owner can accept & denai booking from booking map. Customer also can get notification from email. Restaurant can see booking list form admin panel & also can see order list & its process.
